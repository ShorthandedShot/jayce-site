✶ SYNCH

Spring 2020

An extension of the collaborative reader 'More Than Just Fiction' created with the intention of existing in the real world. An exercise in branding, worldbuilding, and roleplay, this prototyped website was meant as a collaboration between our group and a fictional research organization we created. This group, SYNCH, focused on ‘unexplained phenomena’ that was explored in the reader.


Unifying elements and major design choices were made as a group and pages were divided amongst the four of us. Inspiration was drawn from thinks tanks and similar organizations in order to create a convincing platform.


in use: adobe xd (prototyping), photoshop, illustrator
