✶ FLUX & Speculation

Fall 2020

Semester long research for a speculative space, leading to the final creation of a speculative artifact. my speculative space was based on a chosen word, which was ‘Fantasy.’ this word was mapped out before moving on to a creation of the artifact based on this research. FLUX is a “worldbuilding program” in the form of a prototype that was created to exist in this space, building upon the ideas of fantasy i wanted to explore.


in use: adobe xd, illustrator, photoshop, blender
