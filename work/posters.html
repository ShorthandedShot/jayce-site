A variety of commissioned posters created during a semester long class.

Posters were created using Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects and Risograph printing. An archive of posters created during all sessions of the class can be found on the Posters Only Instagram.

The fall session concluded with an in-person exhibition that featured a selection of posters from each student, a website, and live music. Exhibition roles were split into different teams and roles—exhibition design, digital poster managers, signage & vinyl, exhibition catalogue, and promo. Photo by Lizzy Shin.
