Collaborations with The Anderson, a gallery in Richmond, VA.

Exhibition and promotional design for Speculative History→Design, an exhibition featuring the work of six fashion design students on view at VCUarts Qatar.

The visual identity was developed with a focus on highlighting the diverse garments while having an overarching show identity. Given the themes of the show, the arrow motif was developed to enhance it's message while acting as a visual connection throughout. Each wall vinyl design corresponds with the respective artist, utilizing their provided garment flats enlarged to an abstract level.
