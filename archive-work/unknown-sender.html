✶ unknown_sender

Fall 2021

you receive a file from an unknown, yet strangely familiar digital presence who has been trying to reach you...


a conversation between myself and a player character of my own creation in the form of a visual novel type game. players take on the role of me, jayce, as they navigate a short dialogue. in many ways, this is a conversation with a part of myself.


in use: ren'py

✶ unknown_sender_userguide

in-game

  • click or press spacebar in order to advance dialogue
  • click back in order to reverse dialogue
  • player choices are shown in large white rectangles – click on a dialogue choice to advance
  • some paths will result in the program quitting – this is intentional
  • to view previous dialogue, click history
  • options such as text speed can be adjusted under preferences
