✶ about

21 ✶ he/him ✶ richmond, va

Possibility and potential. These are two ideas I often explore in my work, particularly in response to my own reality. Let’s face it, reality kind of sucks sometimes—why should I have to limit myself to this world when there are so many more to be explored within my imagination?

Leaning into my inner thoughts, experiences, and dreams, I use digital platforms and mediums to bring these concepts to life. Heavily influenced by my relationships with fiction and virtual spaces growing up, I enjoy creating new narratives and exploring alternate possibilities through a different lens.
